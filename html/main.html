Research interests

I'm interested in topics in signal processing and machine learning that overlap with applied mathematics, and in particular with optimization, inverse problems, mathematical statistics, and functional analysis. In each of my applied projects, I strive to make the complete link between an engineering application and the right mathematical toolset. My current focus is on inverse problems and their application to biomedical imaging. Previously, I have been involved in projects concerning wireless communications and the foundations of privacy-preserving data analysis.
