Research Biography

Academic degrees and employment

In September 2019, I obtained a Teknologie doktorsexamen inom elektro- och systemteknik (Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden). Before that, I obtained a double 5-year engineering degree, Enginyer de Telecomunicació (Telecommunications Engineering, UPC BarcelonaTech, Barcelona, Catalonia) and Civilingejör inom Elektroteknik (Electrical Engineering, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden). Since October 2019, I am a research staff scientist at the CIBM Center for Biomedical Imaging, Switzerland, and a postdoctoral researcher at the EPFL's Biomedical Imaging Group, Lausanne, Switzerland.

Academic activity and funds

I am an active reviewer in my fields of interest. During my time at EPFL, I have been awarded CHF 44'000 to lead a project on innovative solutions for remote practical learning. During my Ph.D., I was awarded a number of travel grants amounting to a total of 64'200 SEK. Among others, these grants included the Åforsk Foundation travel grant and the KVA Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences Engineering Sciences grant. Their intent was to support the international promotion of my research in inverse problems in imaging, as well as research visits to Stephen Boyd's research group at the Information Systems Laboratory, Department of Electrical Engineering, Stanford University and to CosmoStat, Jean-Luc Starck's group in CEA Paris-Saclay.
